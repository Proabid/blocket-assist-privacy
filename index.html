Blocket Assist Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2024

1. Introduction

Blocket Assist ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email address (for premium subscription management)
  • Authentication tokens (for secure access to premium features)

2.2 Website Content

  • Blocket listing data (titles, descriptions, prices, images)

2.3 Usage Data

  • Feature usage statistics
  • Error reports
  • Extension performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining the extension's core functionality
  • Managing premium subscriptions
  • Improving the extension's performance and features
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited data retention periods

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • Stripe: For payment processing (no payment data is stored by us)
  • Google Analytics: For basic usage analytics

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
